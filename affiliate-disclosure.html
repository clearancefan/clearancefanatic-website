What Is an Affiliate Disclosure?

The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) requires websites to disclose when they have a financial relationship with companies whose products they feature. This page explains how Clearance Fanatic LLC earns money and how that may affect the content you see on clearancefanatic.com.

How We Make Money

Clearance Fanatic is an affiliate marketing website. This means:

  • We partner with retailers through affiliate programs including Commission Junction (CJ), Impact, ShareASale, Rakuten Advertising, and Amazon Associates.
  • When you click a product link and make a purchase, we may receive a commission from the retailer.
  • The commission is paid by the retailer — not by you. You pay the same price you would pay going directly to the retailer.
  • Commission rates vary by retailer and category, typically ranging from 1% to 20% of the purchase price.

How This Affects Our Content

  • Products displayed on our site come from affiliate partner feeds. We do not manually select individual products.
  • Our automated system aggregates clearance and sale items based on price data from retailers.
  • We do not guarantee the accuracy of pricing, availability, or discount information.
  • Clicking a product link and purchasing through it financially benefits Clearance Fanatic LLC.

Our Commitment to You

  • We only display products that appear genuinely discounted based on retailer pricing data.
  • We never accept payment to feature specific products or manipulate search results.
  • We clearly label any sponsored content if it is ever introduced.
